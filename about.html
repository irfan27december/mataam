Our Story

Authentic Arabian flavours, crafted with passion and tradition

Who We Are

Mataam Al Mandi is a celebration of authentic Arabian cuisine, bringing the rich heritage of traditional Mandi cooking to Australia.

Our recipes are inspired by centuries-old Yemeni traditions, using aromatic spices, slow-cooking techniques, and premium ingredients to deliver unforgettable flavours.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become Australia's most loved destination for authentic Arabian dining experiences.

Mission: To serve high-quality, flavour-rich dishes with exceptional hospitality, creating memorable moments for every guest.
